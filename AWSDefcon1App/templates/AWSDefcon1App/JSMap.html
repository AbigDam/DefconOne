(Tutorial is not complete, contribute by asking questions to a moderator via the profiles tab)
• Defeat every nation that is a major nation or another player (see: "How to defeat a nation")
• This is listed in the "Defeat these nations to win the game:" list
• Click on the nation, and then click "Declare War"
• When you reclick you can attack them using either
• Military (see: "How to win military battles")
• or Espionage (see "How to win espionage attacks")
• A "normal ground attack" will attack a nation from you or your ally's border. It is won by a bit of luck and mostly having a larger army (see: "How do I grow my army")
• An "attempted encirclement attack" will lead to either you or you're enemy randomly taking much more casulties, but victory is still detirmed by having a larger army (see: "How do I grow my army")
• A "naval invasion" will land on your enemies beaches, this can be done when you don't border an enemy, but requires having more boats then an enemy (see: "How do I grow my army")
• You can also use planes to lower you're enemies numbers of sodliers, planes, or boats
• Planes can also be used to drop nuclear bombs, though it only works when you have many more planes than your enemy ("See how to get nukes")
• A nation will automatically surrender (you get all their land) when it has been nuked a certain number of times, this is based on population
• All espionage attacks have at max a 90% chance of succeding
• Most attacks requires having double your enemies spies to reach 90% (see: "How to get more spies)
• The "revolt" attack requires having tripple your enemies spies to reach 90% (see: "How to get more spies)
• The "civil war" attack requires having four times your enemies spies to reach 90% (see: "How to get more spies)
• You can never know how many spies your enemy has due to the fog of war
• Launch "recruiting double agents" attacks on smaller countries
• Buy spies using points (see "How to get points")
• Getting aid from allies (see "How to maximize allies Aid")
• Your "nuke time" is the time left before you get you're next nuke
• You can lower "nuke time" by spending points
• You can also lower "nuke time" by getting aid from allies
• You will also see it go down automatically as time passes
• Gain more states to train soldiers (and boats/plants) automatically
• Spend points on buying stuff (see "How to get points")
• Get aid from allies (see "How to maximize allies Aid")
• You get 1 point automatically every turn
• Get points from allies aid(see "How to maximize allies Aid")
• If you don't have an alliance (Nothing written under alliance name) your aid is random and you can't maximize it
• If you have an alliance, the more allies you have, the more aid you will gets
• Get allies by offering alliance to low-hostility nations (check hostility after clicking a nation)
• Get low-hostility nations by giving away resources to nations you want to ally with
